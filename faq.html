Ordering Questions

You can place orders in three ways:

  1. Online: Browse our products page, add items to your cart, and proceed through checkout.
  2. Phone: Call our sales team at +256 700 123456 during business hours.
  3. In-person: Visit our farm store at 123 Organic Farm Road, Kampala.

Online orders can be placed 24/7.

For delivery orders within Kampala, we have a minimum order of UGX 50,000 to ensure efficient delivery operations. There's no minimum for:

  • Pickup orders at our farm
  • Wholesale accounts
  • Special event orders

Order modifications are possible within 1 hour of placement for online orders. After this window:

  • Before processing: Call us immediately at +256 700 123456 - we may be able to help
  • During processing: Changes cannot be made but you can refuse delivery

For wholesale orders, please contact your account manager for modifications.

Product Questions

Yes! All GreenHarvest products meet strict organic standards:

  • Uganda Organic Certification: Certified by UOCB since 2017 (Certificate #UOC-45231)
  • International Standards: Selected products also carry ECOCERT and USDA Organic certifications

We're proud to be one of Uganda's few fully-certified organic farms. You can view our certificates at our farm or view them online.

Proper storage maximizes freshness and shelf life:

Vegetables
  • Leafy greens: Damp cloth in crisper (3-5 days)
  • Root vegetables: Cool, dark place (1-2 weeks)
  • Tomatoes: Countertop until ripe, then fridge
Fruits
  • Avocados: Ripen on counter, refrigerate when ripe
  • Passion fruits: Room temperature (1 week)
  • Bananas: Separate stems, avoid refrigeration

All produce is best consumed within 3 days of delivery for peak freshness.
