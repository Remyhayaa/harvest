Our Story

Nurturing Uganda's Organic Future

Founded in 2015, GreenHarvest Uganda began as a small family farm in Kampala with a vision to provide chemical-free, nutritious food to our community.

  • Certified organic by Uganda Organic Certification Bureau
  • Sustainable farming practices that protect the environment
  • Direct from farm to table for maximum freshness
  • Supporting local farmers and communities
