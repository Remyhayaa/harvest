Our Journey

Milestones in organic farming excellence

2015: Humble Beginnings

June 2015

Started with just 2 acres of land and a vision to provide chemical-free produce to our local community in Kampala.

2017: Organic Certification

March 2017

Received our first organic certification from the Uganda Organic Certification Bureau, validating our sustainable practices.

2019: National Expansion

January 2019

Expanded our delivery network to serve customers across Uganda, growing to 20 acres of certified organic farmland.

2023: 50+ Team Members

Present

Now employing over 50 local team members and supporting 15 smallholder farmers through our organic partnership program.
